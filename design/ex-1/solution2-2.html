So What is This About?

Notice how the text in this section is wider and bold, showcasing how variable fonts allow more flexibility in font styles. This is controlled by the "wdth" and "wght" attributes of the Roboto Flex font.

Participation

Variable fonts let you adjust weights, widths, and other properties with precision. For example, observe the slanted title for the "Road to Enlightenment" section, which is achieved by changing the "slnt" value of the font.
