Understanding Typographic Hierarchy

Typography is the art of arranging text to make written content clear, appealing, and effective. In this page, we explore the adjustments of font height, spacing, and letter spacing to create an enhanced typographic experience.

Headings and Their Role

Headings define the structure of your content. They serve as visual anchors for readers. Here, we use Merriweather for headings to give a formal and elegant look. Notice the increasing line height as the font size decreases to maintain readability.

Font Adjustments in Body Text

Body text, styled with Open Sans, has the following adjustments:

  • Line Height: 1.8 for comfortable reading.
  • Letter Spacing: 0.3px to improve text clarity.
  • Paragraph Spacing: Additional space between paragraphs to avoid a cluttered appearance.

This paragraph includes extra spacing, making it more distinct and providing better visual separation. Notice how this aids readability, especially in dense content.
