Core Design Principles

Design principles are the fundamental ideas and guidelines that designers use to create effective and visually appealing compositions. Understanding these principles is crucial for creating designs that not only look good but also effectively communicate their intended message.

The key principles include balance, contrast, hierarchy, alignment, proximity, repetition, and white space. Each principle plays a vital role in creating designs that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional. When applied correctly, these principles work together to create harmony and visual interest in your designs.

Remember that while these principles provide a foundation for good design, they are guidelines rather than strict rules. Understanding when and how to break these principles can lead to innovative and striking designs that capture attention and communicate effectively.
