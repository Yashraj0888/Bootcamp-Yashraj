E-commerce UX Analysis

Flipkart

Design Elements

  • Clean white and blue color scheme
  • Uniform grid layout with product cards
  • Large, prominent product images
  • Left-sided filter panel with collapsible sections
  • Clear price and ratings display

Information Structure

  • Clear category hierarchy
  • Direct product specifications on cards
  • Simplified pricing with discount display
  • Highlighted key features
  • Detailed customer rating breakdown

Amazon

Design Elements

  • Dense layout with compact product cards
  • Yellow and orange accent colors
  • Clear sponsored content marking
  • Multi-tier navigation system
  • Comprehensive filter options

Information Structure

  • Detailed breadcrumb navigation
  • Comprehensive product descriptions
  • Prime membership integration
  • Multiple seller comparison
  • Prominent delivery information
